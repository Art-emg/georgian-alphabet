How It Works

This interactive web app helps you learn the Georgian alphabet by substituting Russian letters with their Georgian equivalents. Simply enter your Russian text, select the Georgian letters you want to practice, and click 'Transliterate'. The app will convert your text, allowing you to see how the Georgian alphabet works in practice.

The app provides a user-friendly interface with clear instructions and easy-to-use controls. You can select individual letters or use the 'Select All' and 'Clear' buttons to manage your choices. Additional options include generating random text or facts about Georgia to enhance your learning experience.

The main practice area features input and output text boxes, allowing you to easily compare your original text with the transliterated version. You can adjust the text size for better readability and use the 'Transliterate' and 'Clear' buttons to manage your text.

The app also includes optional pronunciation tips to help you understand how each Georgian letter sounds. This feature is designed to make your learning process more comprehensive and effective.

Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your skills, this Georgian Alphabet Trainer is a valuable tool for mastering the Georgian script. Start your journey today and unlock the beauty of the Georgian language.
